My Story

I grew up in Diverse Central New Jersey surrounded by New York, Philadelphia and the Jersey Shore. I attended a Private Boarding Highschool, The Peddie School, where I was exposed to my interests in Computer Science as a participant on the Robotics Team for 3 years. My team won 2nd in our regional tournaments my Junior and Senior years.

I started attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio in 2015, where I was attracted to their strong Engineering Program and Unique and International student body.

At CWRU I have learned to push my limits and succeed in the face of adversity. Being able to see myself accomplish tasks I never thought I could has kept me motivated to learn as much as I can at my time at CWRU.
